Abi
Lead Trainer

With almost 10 years’ experience as a qualified PT, Abi’s has an insatiable passion for his “calling”. His mission (which he has chosen to accept): to invite the world – starting with Wandsworth – into the personal training fitness space and to unlock the door to the results that come from understanding your body’s unique needs.

Abi’s Personal Trainer journey started in 2010 at Fred’s Fitness training in Battersea, where his credibility as a PT and work ethic allowed him to quickly progress up the ranks to studio manager in a short space of time.

His in-depth expertise, customized approach and unquestionable commitment to his clientele has rewarded the business with a fiercely loyal client base that shows no sign of slowing:

“I always Struggled at the Gym before Starting with Abi , Abi showed me how to train, what to eat and has worked with me towards my fitness goals. I have lost 4 stone in a healthy and sustainable way as a result! I couldn’t recommend Abi’s PT more.”

His training methods are based on multiple movements with weights. Overall his passion and end goals is to provide an exceptional and committed service to suit his diverse clientele.

Corporate Social Responsibility – Community impact

Recognising the need to promote active and healthy lifestyles amongst inner-city youth, Abi also donates his time quarterly to running a free sports day for youth between the ages of 13 and 18, encouraging them to channel their physical abilities into a rewarding community-building activity.

Abi's motivation

“Sports is not only a great way to have fun, it keeps mind, body and soul healthy. Engaging the younger generation in the sports day promotes a sense of focus, discipline and good habits. We’ve seen young people adopt a positive attitude towards teamwork and a sense of community in pursuit of a single goal: to win! And they all do.

I know what sports meant to me at a young age; I hope this initiative can make a lasting life-changing difference for the young people involved.”
